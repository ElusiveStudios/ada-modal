This modal window is ADA compliant, with all elements able to be tabbed to. Pressing enter while on the close or submit buttons will activate it's event. You may also hit escape (esc) to close the modal at anytime.

The background will be visually greyed out and cannot be interacted with while the modal is open. Scrolling is also disabled.
